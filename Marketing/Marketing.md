# SaaS Marketing Strategies

Effective marketing is the lifeblood of any SaaS. Here are the core strategies to grow your product.

## 1. SEO (Search Engine Optimization)
- **Content Marketing**: Write blog posts about the problems your SaaS solves.
- **Programmatic SEO**: Create landing pages for long-tail keywords (e.g., "Best AI for [Niche]").

## 2. Social Proof
- **Testimonials**: Display real user feedback.
- **Case Studies**: Show how your product solved a specific problem for a customer.

## 3. Paid Ads
- **Google Ads**: Target high-intent keywords.
- **Meta Ads**: Target based on interests and behavior.

## 4. Community Building
- Build in public (Twitter/X).
- Launch on Product Hunt and Indie Hackers.
